About the initiative

What is EcoConnect?

A youth-led platform that turns environmental data into practical action through education, real-time reporting, and grassroots mobilization.

EcoConnect combines technology, environmental education, and community action to drive grassroots change. Through our mobile app, residents access real-time data, practical learning materials, and local environmental reports.

Youth Eco‚ÄëAmbassadors bridge the gap between digital tools and lived realities. They teach households, facilitate cleanups, and support neighbours to use the app for reporting issues, learning, and tracking progress.

A data dashboard visualises trends and pollution hotspots, while AI helps generate predictive alerts. By translating complex data into accessible knowledge, EcoConnect empowers communities to protect their environment, make informed decisions, and act sustainably.

Why EcoConnect exists

In underserved communities, pollution and waste mismanagement are everyday realities. Residents face overflowing drains, open dumping, and burning of waste ‚Äì yet lack accessible information, tools, and data to respond.

Occasional cleanups and distant policies rarely lead to lasting behaviour change. EcoConnect offers a different approach: co‚Äëcreating solutions with the community, building local capacity, and ensuring that data and decisions are shaped by those most affected.

The EcoConnect platform

From reports to risk alerts ‚Äì all in one eco‚Äësystem

A mobile app for residents, a web dashboard for partners, and Eco Ambassadors connecting it all on the ground.

Designed with communities, the EcoConnect app makes environmental action simple, multilingual, and rewarding. Residents can learn, play, report issues and follow up on progress ‚Äì all from their phones.

  • Education hub: bite‚Äësized lessons and action tips on waste, air quality, water, and climate resilience.
  • Reporting & surveys: residents log pollution hotspots, blocked drains, and illegal dumping with photos and simple categories.
  • Games & challenges: gamified quizzes and community challenges that reward learning and participation.
  • Community forum: conversations, feedback, and collaboration between residents, Eco Ambassadors and partners.
  • AI‚Äëassisted alerts: patterns in reports trigger risk alerts and recommendations for local authorities and community leaders.
